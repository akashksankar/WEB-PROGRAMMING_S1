college of engineering vadakara

The College of Engineering, Vadakara (CEV), established in 1999, is a government-run institution in the Kozhikode district of Kerala . It was the first engineering college to be founded under the Co-operative Academy of Professional Education (CAPE), an initiative of the Government of Kerala. Situated on a serene, 26-acre campus at Kakkoramala, CEV offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.Tech courses in Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Electrical and Electronics Engineering, as well as an MCA program. Initially affiliated with the Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), the college is now affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). In line with its vision to provide quality education to meritorious and economically backward students, the college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and is known for its meritorious admission process based on the Kerala Engineering, Architecture, and Medical Entrance Exam (KEAM).
